# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
People in Thailand requiring legal assistance, including individuals and businesses seeking litigation services, legal consultations, and business registration.

## Product Purpose
To provide a professional, trustworthy, and accessible online presence for "สำนักกฎหมายเที่ยงธรรมทนายความ" (Thieng Tham Lawyer Office), facilitating lead generation through consultations and providing legal knowledge to the public.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Trustworthy, Traditional, and Authoritative. The design uses a deep navy and warm gold palette to evoke a sense of established expertise and reliability.

## Anti-references
- SaaS-like designs with overly rounded corners and playful illustrations.
- "Ambulance chaser" style aggressive red/yellow palettes.
- Generic stock photos of gavels and scales (unless used tastefully and sparingly).
- Overly complex layouts that obscure essential contact information.

## Design Principles
- **Expert Confidence**: Every element should reflect the office's 19+ years of experience.
- **Accessible Expertise**: Legal information should be presented clearly without excessive jargon.
- **Direct Path to Counsel**: Contacting the office should be effortless and prominent.
- **Bilingual Clarity**: Seamless transition between Thai and English content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
- High contrast for readability (Gold/Navy/Ivory).
- Responsive design for all devices.
