# Design

## Palette

- **Navy (Primary)**: `oklch(0.28 0.055 250)` - Deep professional foundation.
- **Gold (Accent)**: `oklch(0.72 0.11 82)` - Trust and prestige.
- **Ivory (Background)**: `oklch(0.972 0.012 84)` - Sophisticated off-white.
- **Charcoal (Ink)**: `oklch(0.27 0.015 40)` - High-contrast text.

## Typography

- **Heading**: `var(--font-heading)` (Th Sarabun New) - Traditional Thai legal aesthetic.
- **Body**: `var(--font-sans)` (Tahoma/Leelawadee UI) - Clean and readable.
- **Mono**: `var(--font-mono)` (Consolas) - For technical data if needed.

## Components

- **Header**: 3-layer structure (Top Info, Branding, Navigation).
- **Mega Menu**: 4-column layout for Services.
- **Cards**: Minimal rounding (8px-12px), subtle borders or shadows, no "ghost-card" pattern.
- **Buttons**: Gold gradient or solid Navy/Gold depending on context.

## Layout

- **Container**: Max-width with consistent padding.
- **Grid**: 2D grid for services and team sections.
- **Spacing**: Generous vertical rhythm (py-16 to py-22).
